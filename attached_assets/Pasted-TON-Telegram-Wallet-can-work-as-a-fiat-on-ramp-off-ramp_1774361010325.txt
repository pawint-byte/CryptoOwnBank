TON + Telegram Wallet can work as a fiat on-ramp/off-ramp bridge, and it's actually one of the more user-friendly options in 2026 for the exact flow you're describing.
But it's not a perfect "scan QR → pay fiat → automatically get RLUSD in your non-custodial wallet with zero extra steps" solution. It gets you 70-80% of the way there with very low friction for the customer, but the bridging part adds a couple of extra clicks and some fees/slippage.
How the flow would actually work right now (March 2026)

Customer buys TON with fiat (this part is excellent)
Customer scans your MyOwnPOS QR/paylink.
They open Telegram Wallet (or Wallet in Telegram / @wallet bot).
They buy TON directly with card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, bank transfer, or local methods (powered by MoonPay, Onramper, Alchemy Pay, Guardarian, etc.).
This is fully built-in, works in 100+ countries (including US), and is one of the smoothest fiat on-ramps available today.

Bridge TON to your preferred crypto (RLUSD / XRP / XLM)
Customer now has TON.
They use a bridge/aggregator inside Telegram or a mini-app (Rubic, STON.fi + cross-chain tools, or TON → EVM bridges that then go to XRPL via Axelar/Squid).
They send the bridged asset (or swap to RLUSD) directly to the wallet address you provide in the paylink.
You receive RLUSD (or whatever you set) in your non-custodial XRPL wallet → it auto-registers in the OwnCoin POS dashboard exactly like any other payment.

Customer doesn't keep TON (exactly as you said)
Most people treat TON as the "temporary on-ramp rail" — buy it, bridge/swap immediately, done. No long-term holding needed.

Realistic verdict for your site

This is a great additional payment option — especially for freelancers/merchants who want to accept payments from Telegram-heavy users (huge in emerging markets and crypto communities).
It’s not a full replacement for a dedicated fiat-to-RLUSD ramp (Alchemy Pay / NOWPayments / Transak) because the bridging step isn’t automatic.
However, it’s much easier to add today than waiting for MoonPay approval — you can literally launch a “Pay with TON (fiat on-ramp)” button in MyOwnPOS in a day or two.

Bonus: The TON Foundation just launched TON Pay SDK (Feb 2026) specifically for merchants and mini-apps. It lets you accept TON/USDT payments directly in Telegram with one-tap checkout. You could combine that with the fiat buy flow for an even smoother experience

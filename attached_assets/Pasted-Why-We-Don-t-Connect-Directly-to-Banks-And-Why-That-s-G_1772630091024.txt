Why We Don’t Connect Directly to Banks (And Why That’s Great for You)
At CryptoOwnBank, we’ve made a deliberate choice: we never touch fiat, never link to your bank account, and never act as a middleman.
Here’s why that matters — and why it actually puts you in the strongest possible position.
1. Maximum Security & True Ownership
When you connect your cold wallet (Ledger or Xumm), your private keys never leave your device.
We never see them, store them, or have any ability to move your funds.
Direct bank integrations (ACH, wires, Plaid-style linking) require someone — us or a third party — to handle your fiat money and personal banking details. That introduces:

KYC requirements
Potential data breaches
Counterparty risk (what if the service gets hacked or freezes funds?)

By staying 100% on-chain, we eliminate all of that.
Your assets stay in your control, not ours or anyone else’s.
That’s what “be your own bank” really means.
2. No Regulatory Overload = Faster, Simpler Experience for You
If we added bank transfers, we would be required to register as a money transmitter (federal MSB + state licenses in 40+ states), implement full AML/KYC programs, hold large surety bonds, and face ongoing audits.
That would mean:

You’d need to submit ID, proof of address, and banking info to use the site
Slower onboarding (days or weeks instead of seconds)
Higher fees to cover compliance costs
Potential account freezes or restrictions if something looks “suspicious”

We refuse to go that route.
Instead, we keep things non-custodial and permissionless — exactly how the blockchain was designed to work.
You get instant access, no middleman, and no one asking for your passport.
3. Lower Costs & Better Yields for You
Every layer of fiat handling adds fees (bank wires, ACH, compliance overhead, partner cuts).
By keeping everything on-chain (RLUSD → Soil vaults → interest withdrawals), we avoid those costs entirely.
Result:

You keep more of your 5–8% fixed yield
No hidden “processing” or “withdrawal” fees from us
The system stays lean so we can focus on adding features like auto-withdrawals and XLS-66 lending — not compliance paperwork

How It Actually Works (Simple & Safe)

Buy RLUSD
Use any trusted exchange (Binance, Kraken, Coinbase, etc.).
Buy RLUSD → withdraw it directly to your XRPL wallet address.
(We provide affiliate links so you may earn rewards for using them — disclosed transparently.)
Connect Your Cold Wallet
Scan with Xumm or plug in Ledger.
Takes seconds — no forms, no ID, no waiting.
Deposit to Soil Vaults
Choose a vault (Treasury-backed 5.2% or Private Credit 7.8%).
Sign the transaction with your cold wallet.
Your RLUSD is now earning fixed yield — principal stays locked and protected.
Withdraw Only the Interest
When earnings accrue, click “Withdraw Interest Only”.
The app calculates and sends only the interest to your chosen spending wallet (another XRPL address you control).
Your principal never moves.
Repeat Forever
Your base stays intact and continues earning.
You live off the interest — or compound it — without ever selling your holdings.

No banks. No middlemen. No permission needed.
Just you, your keys, and the XRPL doing what it was built for.
Bottom Line
We don’t connect to banks because you don’t need us to.
You already have full control — and that’s exactly how we want to keep it.
CryptoOwnBank is here to make the on-chain experience easier and more powerful, not to become another gatekeeper.
Your keys. Your funds. Your bank.

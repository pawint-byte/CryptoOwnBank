FAQ Entry: Can CryptoOwnBank Connect to Other Blockchains and Protocols in the Future?
Yes — and that’s exactly the vision.
CryptoOwnBank is built to be your personal on-chain control center — not just for XRPL today, but for any blockchain or smart contract that offers valuable capabilities (yield, lending, staking, tokenized assets, etc.).
How it works (and why it’s safe)
Whenever a new protocol or blockchain exposes features through smart contracts or on-chain rules, we can plug in by:

Reading public blockchain data (balances, yields, positions) via open RPC nodes or indexers
Building ready-to-sign transactions that your cold wallet (Ledger or Xumm) approves
You sign and broadcast directly — we never touch your keys or funds

This is the same non-custodial model we use today for RLUSD → Soil vaults. It keeps you in full control while we handle the complexity.
Why this is the future of “being your own bank”
In the old world, banks controlled everything: your money, the interface, the rules.
In the blockchain world, you control the money — but the raw experience is still clunky (manual transaction building, multiple wallets, confusing explorers).
We bridge that gap:

One beautiful dashboard instead of scattered apps and command lines
One-click actions instead of copy-pasting addresses
Automation (auto-withdraw interest, alerts, scheduled moves) without giving up sovereignty
Future-proof expansion: When XLS-66 XRP lending activates, or when new RWA protocols launch on XRPL (or even multi-chain), we add them — you get instant access without switching tools

You stay the owner. We become your smart assistant — making self-custody powerful, convenient, and actually enjoyable.

1. XRPL Native AMM Liquidity Pools (Most Permissionless & Immediate Option)

How it works: Use the built-in XRPL Automated Market Maker (AMM, live since 2024/2025 upgrades). Deposit RLUSD + XRP (or RLUSD + another token) into a liquidity pool. You get LP tokens representing your share. Earn a direct cut of all trading fees generated in that pool — purely on-chain, no intermediaries.
Member power: 100% yours. Wallet-only interaction. Withdraw anytime (subject to pool rules). No company manages your funds; the ledger handles it atomically. Fees compound in real-time as volume grows.
RLUSD-specific yields: RLUSD/XRP pools are active and one of the top ways to earn on stablecoins right now. Yields scale with trading volume (currently modest but real; expect growth with RLUSD adoption). No fixed APR, but it's organic and risk is mainly impermanent loss (lower for stablecoin pairs).
Why new/underused? XRPL AMM is still ramping up compared to Ethereum/Solana. Many users stick to simple holding or Soil; LP is "unused" by retail but institutions are eyeing it for RLUSD flows.
How to access: XRPL wallets (Xaman, etc.) or DEX interfaces. No bridging.
Risks: Impermanent loss, smart contract-like ledger risks (very low on XRPL).

This is the purest "hold and earn" without leaving XRPL or trusting anyone.
2. Tellement-French (or Similar Native Lending UIs on XLS-65/66 Vaults)

How it works: Built 100% on XRPL's native lending primitives (Single Asset Vaults + Lending Protocol/XLS-66, now live). Deposit RLUSD or XRP into public/native vaults. Earn yield from borrower interest (fixed-term loans). Borrow against collateral with on-chain liquidation. Real-time APY tracking and one-click deposit/withdraw.
Member power: Fully ledger-native — no smart contracts, no Hooks, no third-party custody. Everything is on-ledger transactions. Vault owners/brokers handle underwriting (some off-chain risk mgmt), but public vaults are permissionless for depositors. You control your position directly; first-loss capital protects depositors in defaults.
RLUSD fit: Explicitly supports RLUSD deposits for yield. This is one of the first full "DeFi experience" apps layered on the new native lending (post-2026 activation).
Why new/underused? Hackathon-born project that's one of the earliest live implementations. XRPL lending is brand new, so TVL is low and adoption is early — perfect "unused" gem for early members.
Access: Check XRPL Commons hackathon projects or XRPL explorers for the front-end (it's composed of pure ledger txns).

This is the closest native equivalent to Soil but without the company intermediary layer.
3. VS1.Finance (XRPL Institutional DeFi Hub with Yield Strategies)

How it works: All-in-one XRPL platform for trading (DEX access), yield optimization, and RWA plays. AI-driven analytics and auto-deployment into liquidity/yield opportunities (including AMM pools and emerging vaults). Supports RLUSD flows.
Member power: Non-custodial wallet connection. Focuses on decentralized markets with on-chain execution. It's designed as a "smarter DeFi layer" for XRPL — you stay in control while AI helps maximize yields.
Why new/underused? Launched ~late 2025/early 2026 as a comprehensive hub. Still building adoption in the XRPL ecosystem (low visibility vs. Ethereum giants).
Access: vs1.finance — connects directly to XRPL wallets.

Ethereum-Side for RLUSD (Since RLUSD Is Native on ETH Too — Fully Permissionless Classic DeFi)
RLUSD works seamlessly on Ethereum DeFi without bridging from XRPL in many cases.

Aave (especially Horizon/RWA markets): Supply RLUSD to earn variable yield (currently in 3-6%+ range depending on utilization). Fully permissionless, non-custodial, governed by AAVE DAO. Recent integrations make RLUSD collateral in tokenized RWA flows. No gatekeepers — code runs everything.
Morpho Blue (Modular/Permissionless Lending): Newer, highly customizable. Anyone (including you) can create isolated lending markets for RLUSD. Immutable, no governance intervention. Extremely member-powered — vaults are user-created and controlled via smart contracts. Great for tailored RLUSD yields with low fees. Underutilized for RLUSD specifically but exploding in broader DeFi.

These are more mature than XRPL options but give true sovereignty.

2. How to Transfer RLUSD from Uphold

Log in to Uphold (app or web).
Go to Withdraw or Send → select RLUSD.
Choose Crypto network (XRPL).
Enter your XRPL wallet address (from Xaman or Ledger — make sure you've already set up the RLUSD trust line).
Confirm low XRPL network fees (~0.0001 XRP).
Submit — funds arrive in seconds to minutes.
Tip: Test with a small amount first (1–10 RLUSD) to avoid mistakes.


Before transferring: Add the RLUSD trust line in Xaman (or your wallet app): "+" → Add Token → RLUSD → Setup Trust Line → sign on your hardware wallet.

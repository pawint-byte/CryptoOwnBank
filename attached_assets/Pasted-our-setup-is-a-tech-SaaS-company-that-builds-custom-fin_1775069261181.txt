1. Quick prep (do this first – takes 1–2 days)
You’ll need:

Your EIN (from IRS).
Articles/Certificate of Incorporation for Wint Enterprises Inc.
Driver’s license or passport of the authorized signer(s) (you).
Business address (Reston, VA is fine).
A short business description: “Technology company providing enterprise-grade digital legacy and financial control tools, including a non-custodial crypto portfolio and yield platform on XRPL/Stellar.”

Tip: Be honest but frame it as “non-custodial SaaS fintech” — banks love that wording because it avoids MSB (money services business) flags.
